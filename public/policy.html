All bookings made through Bahamas Stays are subject to availability and operator confirmation.

Booking Process

When you submit a booking request through Bahamas Stays, you will receive an immediate booking confirmation via email. Your booking is confirmed upon receipt of this confirmation email and successful payment processing.

Pricing & Taxes

All prices displayed on Bahamas Stays are in US dollars. Prices are subject to Bahamian VAT (10%) and a tourism levy of $10 per night per room. These charges are clearly displayed during the booking process and included in your total.

  • Bahamas VAT: 10% applied to all accommodation charges
  • Tourism Levy: $10 per night, per room
  • Platform Service Fee: Included in displayed pricing

Payment

Full payment is required at the time of booking. We accept major credit and debit cards processed through our secure payment partners. All transactions are processed in US dollars.

Booking Modifications

Requests to modify existing bookings (change dates, room type, or number of guests) are subject to availability and operator approval. Please contact us at bookings@bahamasstays.com to request modifications.

Operator Commission

Bahamas Stays charges property operators a platform commission of 8% for Founding Partners (first 20 operators) and 10% for standard partners. This commission is deducted from operator payouts and is not an additional charge to guests.

Bahamas Stays includes hurricane protection on all bookings — a benefit unique to our Bahamian-built platform.

Standard Cancellation Policy

Our standard cancellation policy applies to all bookings unless otherwise stated on the property listing:

  • More than 7 days before check-in: Full refund
  • 3–7 days before check-in: 50% refund
  • Less than 72 hours before check-in: No refund
  • No-show: No refund

Hurricane & Tropical Storm Protection

Bahamas Stays provides full booking flexibility in the event of a named hurricane or tropical storm warning issued by the Bahamas Department of Meteorology that directly affects your destination island.

In such circumstances, guests may:

  • Rebook for any available future dates at no additional cost
  • Receive a full credit valid for 24 months
  • Receive a full refund at the operator's discretion

Hurricane protection applies when a tropical storm or hurricane warning is in effect for your destination within 48 hours of your scheduled check-in date.

How to Cancel

To cancel a booking, log in to your Bahamas Stays account, navigate to My Bookings, and select Cancel Booking. Alternatively, contact us at bookings@bahamasstays.com with your booking reference number.

Refund Processing

Eligible refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or card issuer.

Operator-Specific Policies

Some properties may have more flexible or more restrictive cancellation policies, which will be clearly stated on the property listing page. In the event of a conflict between a property-specific policy and this general policy, the property-specific policy applies.
